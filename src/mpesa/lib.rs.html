2//!
3//! ## Optional Features
4//!
5//! The following are a list of [Cargo features][cargo-features] that can be
6//! enabled or disabled:
7//!
8//! - **account_balance** *(enabled by default)*: Enables Account Balance service support.
9//! - **b2b** *(enabled by default)*: Enables B2B service support.
10//! - **b2c** *(enabled by default)*: Enables B2C service support.
11//! - **bill_manager** *(enabled by default)*: Enables Bill Manager services support.
12//! - **c2b_register** *(enabled by default)*: Enables C2B URL Registration service support.
13//! - **c2b_simulate** *(enabled by default)*: Enables C2B request simulation service support.
14//! - **express** *(enabled by default)*: Enables Mpesa Express (STK push) service support.
15//! - **dynamic_qr** *(enabled by default)*: Enables Dynamic QR generation service support.
16//! - **transaction_reversal** *(enabled by default)*: Enables Transaction Reversal service support.
17//! - **transaction_status** *(enabled by default)*: Enables Transaction Status service support.
18//! - **no_openssl** *(enabled by default)*: Disables the dependency on `openssl` as the crate for handling mpesa
19//!   certificates and base64 encoding, instead using the `x509-parser`, `rsa`, `base64` and `rand` crates.
20//!   Automatically enabled when any of the features *account_balance*, *b2b*, *b2c*, *bill_manager*, *express*,
21//!   *transaction_reversal*, or *transaction_status* are enabled.
22//! - **openssl**: Enables the use of `openssl` as the dependency for handling certificates and base64 encoding instead
23//!   of the default.
