1//!# MPESA Services
2//!
3//! Using the builder pattern in this to procedurally build Mpesa service specific payloads which are
4//! ultimately consumed and the request sent by calling the `send` method.
5//! Some of the builder methods for certain services are optional with default values standing in
6//! their place when the builder gets consumed
7//!
8//! Here are the currently supported services:
9//! 1. [Account Balance](https://developer.safaricom.co.ke/APIs/AccountBalance)
10//! 2. [B2B](https://developer.safaricom.co.ke/APIs/BusinessPayBill)
11//! 3. [B2C](https://developer.safaricom.co.ke/APIs/BusinessToCustomer)
12//! 4. [C2B Register](https://developer.safaricom.co.ke/APIs/CustomerToBusinessRegisterURL)
13//! 5. [C2B Simulate](https://developer.safaricom.co.ke/c2b/apis/post/simulate)
14//! 6. [Mpesa Express/ STK Push](https://developer.safaricom.co.ke/APIs/MpesaExpressSimulate)
15//! 7. [Transaction Reversal](https://developer.safaricom.co.ke/Documentation)
16//! 8. [Bill Manager](https://developer.safaricom.co.ke/APIs/BillManager)
17//! 9. [Transaction Status](https://developer.safaricom.co.ke/APIs/TransactionStatus)
18//! 10. [Dynamic QR](https://developer.safaricom.co.ke/APIs/DynamicQRCode)
19
